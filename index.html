About Call of Duty

Call of Duty is a first-person shooter franchise that started in 2003. Over the years, it has become one of the most iconic gaming series in the world, spanning numerous titles, settings, and gameplay modes.

From World War II battles to futuristic warfare, CoD offers something for every gamer.

All Call of Duty Games

  • Call of Duty (2003): The game that started it all, focusing on World War II.
  • Call of Duty 2 (2005): Improved graphics and a continuation of the WWII setting.
  • Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare (2007): Introduced modern combat and set the stage for the iconic series.
  • Call of Duty: Black Ops (2010): A cold war-themed game with a gripping story and zombies mode.
  • Call of Duty: Warzone (2020): A free-to-play battle royale with cross-platform play.

How to Play and Tips

Beginner Tips

  • Focus on learning the map layouts to gain a tactical advantage.
  • Choose weapons that match your playstyle, such as SMGs for close combat or sniper rifles for long-range.
  • Stick with your teammates in multiplayer to avoid being an easy target.

Advanced Tips

  • Master the art of "pre-aiming" at common choke points.
  • Use grenades to flush out enemies from hiding spots.
  • Experiment with custom loadouts to find your perfect setup.

Multiplayer Modes

  • Team Deathmatch: A classic mode where two teams compete to get the most kills.
  • Search and Destroy: A tactical mode where one team plants a bomb while the other defends.
  • Domination: Teams capture and hold objectives to score points.
  • Warzone: A battle royale mode where players fight to be the last one standing.

How to Get the Game & Controls

How to Get the Game

You can buy Call of Duty games on popular platforms such as:

  • PC: Available on Battle.net and Steam.
  • PlayStation: Available in the PlayStation Store or retail outlets.
  • Xbox: Available in the Microsoft Store or retail outlets.
  • Mobile: Call of Duty Mobile is free to download on iOS and Android devices.

Controls

PC

  • Move: W, A, S, D
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Button
  • Aim: Right Mouse Button
  • Reload: R
  • Jump: Space

PlayStation

  • Move: Left Stick
  • Shoot: R2
  • Aim: L2
  • Reload: Square
  • Jump: X

Xbox

  • Move: Left Stick
  • Shoot: RT
  • Aim: LT
  • Reload: X
  • Jump: A
